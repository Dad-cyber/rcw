Our Mission

Founded in 2018, RoboComm exists to democratize robotics education and foster collaboration across all skill levels. We believe the next breakthrough in robotics will come from passionate individuals working together.

Our open-source philosophy means every project, tutorial, and resource is available to help you grow as a roboticist while contributing to the community.
